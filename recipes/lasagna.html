Lasagna

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato products
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Lasagna noodles
  • cheeses
  • Egg

Steps

  1. This super meaty lasagna has sweet italian sasuage and lean groud beef.
  2. An onion and two cloves of garlic are cooked with the meat to add tons of flavor.
  3. You'll need a can of curshed tomatoes, two cans of tomato sauce, and two cans of tomato paste.
  4. Two tablespoons of white sugar add suble sweetness and enchance the flavor of the sauce.
  5. This lasagna recipe is flavored with fresh parsley, dried basil leaves, salt, italian seasoning, fennel seeds, and black pepper.
  6. Use store-bought or homemade lasagna noodle
  7. Parmesan, mozzarella, and riccota cheese make this lasagna extra decadent.
  8. An egg helps bind the ricotta so it doesn't ooze our of the lasagna when you cut into it.
